Prambanan Temple

Prambanan (Indonesian: Candi Prambanan, Javanese: Rara Jonggrang, Hanacaraka: ꦫꦫꦗꦺꦴꦁꦒꦿꦁ) is a 9th-century Hindu temple compound in the Special Region of Yogyakarta, in southern Java, Indonesia, dedicated to the Trimūrti, the expression of God as the Creator (Brahma), the Preserver (Vishnu) and the Destroyer (Shiva). The temple compound is located approximately 17 kilometres (11 mi) northeast of the city of Yogyakarta on the boundary between Central Java and Yogyakarta provinces.

Merapi Volcano Museum

The Merapi Volcano Museum ( Javanese : ꦩꦸꦱꦶꦪꦸꦩ꧀ꦒꦸꦤꦸꦁꦩꦼꦫꦥꦶ , translit. Musiyum Gunung Merapi ) is a geological museum located in Hargobinangun , Pakem , Sleman , Special Region of Yogyakarta , Indonesia; precisely about five kilometers south of the Kaliurang tourist area . This museum was inaugurated on October 1, 2009 by the Minister of Energy and Mineral Resources (ESDM), Purnomo Yusgiantoro . The museum occupies an area of ​​3.5 hectares and is equipped with a park, parking area, and plaza; and carries the slogan "Merapi Jendela Bumi "

Obelix Hills

Obelix Hills is not as popular as HeHa Ocean View and HeHa Sky View. In fact, Obelix Hills has a stunning sunset view and 30 photo spots. There are indeed some paid photo spots, but more are free. Even so, Obelix Hills is more suitable for hanging out and enjoying the sunset view than just taking selfies.
